Japan Visa Cost for US Citizens (2025)

If you are a US citizen planning to visit Japan, you may not need a visa for short stays.

Do US Citizens Need a Visa?

US citizens can stay in Japan visa-free for up to 90 days.

Visa Cost

  • Single-entry: ¥3,000
  • Multiple-entry: ¥6,000
  • Transit visa: ¥700

Processing Time

Usually 5 business days.

Required Documents

  • Passport
  • Application form
  • Photo
  • Travel itinerary
