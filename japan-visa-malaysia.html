Japan Visa Requirements for Malaysians (2025)

Malaysian citizens can travel to Japan visa-free for short stays.

Do Malaysians Need a Visa?

No visa is required for stays up to 90 days for tourism or business.

Requirements

  • Valid passport
  • Return ticket
  • Proof of funds

When Visa is Required

  • Work
  • Study
  • Long-term stay

Processing Time

Usually around 5 business days if visa is needed.
