Japan Tourist Visa Requirements (2025)

Japan offers visa-free travel for many countries, but some travelers need a visa.

Who Needs a Visa?

Citizens from countries without visa exemption must apply.

Documents Required

  • Passport
  • Visa application form
  • Photo
  • Travel itinerary
  • Bank statements

Visa Cost

  • Single-entry: ¥3,000
  • Multiple-entry: ¥6,000

Processing Time

Typically 5 business days.
